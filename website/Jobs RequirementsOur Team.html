Jobs Requirements/Our Team

Junior Web Developer

Job Specifications:
1. Web Development Skills: Demonstrated proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
2. Basic Understanding of Web Technologies: Familiarity with frameworks like React, Angular, or Vue.js is a plus.
3. Collaboration: Ability to work in a team environment, collaborating with designers and other developers.

Required Qualifications:
1. Education: A Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field. Relevant coding bootcamps or certifications are also acceptable.
2. Experience: Entry-level applicants are welcome. Prior internship or project experience in web development is advantageous.
3. Technical Proficiency: Solid understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals is essential.
4. Portfolio: A portfolio showcasing coding projects or websites developed independently or as part of coursework.
5. Communication Skills: Good verbal and written communication skills to effectively collaborate within a team.
6. Initiative and Eagerness to Learn: Demonstrated curiosity and eagerness to grow in the field of web development
